## What You Get

- A non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use PrismMCP for the
  duration of your Annual Term (12 months from activation).
- All updates and new versions released during your Annual Term.
- Direct email support with priority triage.

## What Happens After 12 Months

Your license does **not auto-renew.** There is no recurring charge.
When your Annual Term ends, PrismMCP stops working after a 10-day
offline grace window. To continue, you buy a new license at the
then-current price. No surprise charges, no cancellation dance.

## Activation and Offline Use

PrismMCP requires a license key activation and periodic online
verification (roughly weekly). If offline, the plugin continues
working for **10 days** before re-verification is needed.

Data transmitted during activation:

- License key and email address (your identifier)
- Machine fingerprint hash (one-way; no plaintext hardware IDs)
- Plugin version and activation timestamp
- IP address (transient, not stored)

Data **never** transmitted: project paths, file contents, editor
logs, asset names, or hardware specifics beyond the fingerprint hash.

See the [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md) for full details.
